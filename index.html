Avatar

PG-13 | fantasy-sci-fi | 2 HR 43 MIN | 2009

Set in the year 2154, James Cameron's sci-fi epic follows the U.S. military effort to mine an extremely valuable mineral from a distant world called Pandora. When a paraplegic ex-Marine becomes an Avatar to Pandora, he is taken in by a feisty female native and taught the customs. But when romance ensues, he finds himself at the center of the battle for control of Pandora.
